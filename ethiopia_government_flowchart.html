Federal Power (Art. 96)
Exclusive Federal Taxes
  • Customs duties & import taxes
  • Federal employee income tax
  • Federal enterprise taxes
  • Air, rail, sea transport taxes
  • National lottery taxes
💜
Concurrent Power (Art. 98)
Shared Revenue
  • Joint enterprise taxes
  • Private corporate taxes (50/50)
  • Mining & petroleum royalties
  • Large-scale resource taxes

State Power (Art. 97)

  • Land lease fees
  • Agricultural income tax
  • Individual trader taxes
  • Private property taxes

  • Modern property taxes
  • Motor vehicle circulation taxes
  • Any new tax categories
